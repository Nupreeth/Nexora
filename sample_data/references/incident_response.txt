Incident Response Program Summary

CrestPilot maintains a 24x7 on-call incident response rotation for production services.

Severity Model
- SEV-1: Material customer impact or confirmed unauthorized access.
- SEV-2: Service degradation with no evidence of unauthorized access.

Customer Notification Commitments
- For confirmed security incidents affecting customer data, CrestPilot notifies impacted customers without undue delay and within 24 hours of confirmation.
- Follow-up updates are sent at least every 24 hours until containment is completed.

Post-Incident Actions
- Root cause analysis is completed within 10 business days for SEV-1 incidents.
- Corrective actions are tracked to closure by Security and Engineering leadership.
