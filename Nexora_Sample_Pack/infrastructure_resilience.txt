Infrastructure Resilience and Disaster Recovery

Hosting
- Production workloads are hosted on Amazon Web Services in the us-east-1 and us-west-2 regions.
- Core services are deployed across multiple availability zones.

Backups
- Primary PostgreSQL backups run every 6 hours with point-in-time recovery enabled.
- Object storage replication is configured with cross-region copies every 12 hours.
- Backup retention is 35 days.

Recovery Objectives
- Target Recovery Time Objective (RTO) for critical customer-facing systems is 4 hours.
- Target Recovery Point Objective (RPO) is 30 minutes.

Disaster Recovery Testing
- Tabletop disaster recovery exercises occur twice per year.
- Full restoration tests are conducted at least once per year.
