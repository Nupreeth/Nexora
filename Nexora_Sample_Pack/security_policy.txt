CrestPilot Logistics Cloud Security Baseline v4.2

Identity and Authentication
- Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all engineering, platform, and support admin accounts in production.
- SSO is enforced through Okta for all internal users.
- Privileged sessions are logged and reviewed monthly.

Cryptography
- Customer data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 in managed database and object storage services.
- All external and service-to-service traffic uses TLS 1.2+.
- Deprecated ciphers and weak protocols are disabled by policy.

Application Logging
- Application and infrastructure logs are centralized in the security data lake.
- Standard log retention is 365 days online and 90 additional days in cold archive.
- Audit logs for admin actions are retained for 15 months.

Security Training
- Mandatory security awareness training is completed during onboarding.
- All employees complete annual refresher training and quarterly phishing simulations.
