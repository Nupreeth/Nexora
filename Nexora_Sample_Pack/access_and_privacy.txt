Access Control and Data Governance

- Role-based access control is implemented in the application for customer tenant administrators and operators.
- Internal production access uses least-privilege role grants and requires managerial approval.
- Access recertification is completed quarterly.

Data Residency and Subprocessors
- Customer data is primarily hosted in the United States.
- Enterprise customers can request dedicated regional deployment options subject to contract scope.
- CrestPilot maintains a subprocessor register and provides change notifications 30 days before onboarding a new subprocessor.
